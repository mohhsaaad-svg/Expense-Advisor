# BASIRA بصيرة — Brand Kit v1
### Jamil (Design Studio) · Saad Ventures · 2026-07-20
*"Basira" = insight, inner sight, foresight. The brand IS the promise: seeing your money's future clearly.*

## 1. Brand idea
**"The calm eye on your money."** Not a hype fintech, not a shaming budget app — a wise, calm companion that sees ahead. Everything visual says: clarity, foresight, trust, calm.

## 2. Logo direction
- **Mark:** a minimal eye/lens shape where the iris is a crescent-like arc — "the eye that sees ahead." Works at 16px favicon size.
- **Wordmark:** "Basira" latin + "بصيرة" Arabic, equal prominence. Arabic NEVER smaller than English.
- MVP version: text wordmark + the eye glyph (◉ styled). Proper logo commission later.

## 3. Color system
| Role | Color | Hex | Use |
|------|-------|-----|-----|
| Ink (primary) | Deep teal-ink | #0F2E2E | Backgrounds, headers — calm depth |
| Sight (accent) | Luminous teal | #17B8A6 | Brand accent, buttons, links |
| Sand (surface) | Warm sand | #F6F1E8 | Light surfaces, cards — regional warmth |
| SAFE | Confident green | #2FA46B | Safe-to-spend positive state |
| CAUTION | Amber | #E5A63C | Tight/low-confidence state |
| RISK | Calm red | #D8544A | Negative/critical (never screaming) |
| Text-light | Off-white | #F4F6F5 | Text on ink |
| Muted | Grey-teal | #7C9694 | Secondary text |

Rule: the **safe-to-spend number owns the color** (green/amber/red). Everything else stays neutral so the state reads instantly.

## 4. Typography
- **IBM Plex Sans Arabic** (free, Google Fonts) — one family, native Arabic + Latin, professional, excellent numerals.
- Numbers always tabular. The hero number is huge (48–64pt), everything else humble.

## 5. Voice
- Calm, respectful, precise. Arabic-first idioms, no slang, ZERO shame ("Here's your new safe number" — never "you overspent!").
- Every number offers its "why" — transparency is a brand behavior, not a feature.

## 6. Signature UI moments
1. **The Hero Number** — safe-to-spend, huge, colored by state, with "until payday · X days" beneath.
2. **The Why Sheet** — tap the number → the four-line calculation, each line tappable. (Trust ritual.)
3. **The Commitments River** — upcoming commitments as a timeline flowing to payday.
4. **One Action card** — a single, calm recommendation. Never a feed.

## 7. Do / Don't
- ✅ RTL designed first; LTR mirrors it
- ✅ Dark ink theme default (fits region's OLED phones + feels premium)
- ❌ No confetti, streak-fires, or guilt-red walls
- ❌ No dashboards crowded with charts — ONE number rules every screen
